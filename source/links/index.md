# Free Software
- [Defective By Design](https://www.defectivebydesign.org) (defectivebydesign.org) is a participatory and grassroots campaign exposing DRM-encumbered devices and media for what they really are: Defective by Design. We are working together to eliminate DRM as a threat to innovation in media, the privacy of readers, and freedom for computer users.
- [Free Software Foundation](https://www.fsf.org) (fsf.org) is a nonprofit with a worldwide mission to promote computer user freedom.
- [GNU](https://www.gnu.org) (gnu.org) is an operating system that is [free software](https://www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html)—that is, it respects users' freedom.

# Wikimedia
- [Wikipedia](https://www.wikipedia.org) (wikipedia.org) is a free online [encyclopedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Encyclopedia) written and maintained by a community of volunteers, known as [Wikipedians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedians), through [open collaboration](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_collaboration) and the [wiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiki) software [MediaWiki](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MediaWiki).
